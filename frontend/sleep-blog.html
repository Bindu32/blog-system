One of the most effective steps is to create a consistent sleep schedule. Going to bed and waking up at the same time every day helps regulate your circadian rhythm.

Instead, consider reading a physical book, practicing meditation, or listening to calming music. Many people find that journaling before bed helps release the thoughts of the day, making it easier to relax.
